What is daily EMI insurance and how does it work? +
Daily EMI insurance lets you pay your insurance premium in small daily installments instead of large monthly or yearly amounts. For example, instead of paying NPR 30,000 a year, you pay as little as NPR 82/day. We deduct this automatically via your preferred payment method.
Is BeemaDukaan regulated and safe to use? +
Yes. BeemaDukaan is fully compliant with the Insurance Authority of Nepal (Beema Samiti) regulations. We partner only with licensed insurance companies in Nepal. Your policy is issued directly by the insurer — we act as a transparent intermediary.
Can I buy insurance for my whole family under one plan? +
Yes! Our Health Insurance plans offer family floater options where your spouse, children, and even parents can be covered under a single policy. This is often more cost-effective than buying individual policies for each member.
What documents do I need to buy insurance? +
For most plans you only need your Citizenship Certificate (नागरिकता), a passport-size photo, and your phone number. For higher coverage amounts, additional documents like income proof may be required. Our advisors will guide you through the exact requirements for your chosen plan.
How do I file a claim? +
Simply call us at +977 98023 79831 or message us on WhatsApp and we'll guide you through the claim process step by step. We act as your advocate with the insurer to ensure your claim is processed quickly and fairly.
Can I cancel my policy and get a refund? +
Yes, most policies have a free-look period of 15 days during which you can cancel and get a full refund. After that, surrender value depends on the type of plan and how long you've held it. Our advisors will clearly explain the terms before you purchase.
What's the difference between term and life insurance? +
Term insurance gives pure protection — high coverage at the lowest premium, but no payout if you survive the term. Life insurance (whole life or endowment) combines protection with savings, paying out at maturity or on death. Term is ideal for maximum protection at minimum cost; life insurance is better if you want to build savings too.
